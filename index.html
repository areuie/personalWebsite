Hi! I'm Alisa, a MaCS student at William Lyon Mackenzie!

Create
/krēˈāt/
transitive verb

  1. bring (something) into existence.
  2. to produce through imaginative skill

If there is anything that I'd want to do, it'd be to create. I love creating, whether that be artistically or technically. Wherever you go, art and technology will be seen together. They are intertwined. If there is technology, there will always be a accompanying visual. I enjoy graphic design, whether that be with the pen tool on Photoshop and Illustrator or by drawing in designs using pixel art or with digital art. The pen tool is the best thing that has been created, ever —and I stand by that. Oh, and I'm also an up and coming coder (aha), so watch out for my projects as I continue to learn in my quest to create.
